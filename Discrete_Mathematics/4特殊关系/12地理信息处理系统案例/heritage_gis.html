变换公式

正变换 (Forward)

$$f(x,y) = \begin{pmatrix} s \cdot (x\cos\theta - y\sin\theta) \\ s \cdot (x\sin\theta + y\cos\theta) \end{pmatrix}$$

逆变换 (Inverse)

$$f^{-1}(x',y') = \begin{pmatrix} \frac{1}{s}(x'\cos\theta + y'\sin\theta) \\ \frac{1}{s}(-x'\sin\theta + y'\cos\theta) \end{pmatrix}$$
